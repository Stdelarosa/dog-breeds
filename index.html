My Favorite Dog Breeds

Yorkshire Terrier

Bold, confident, and surprisingly brave for their size. Yorkies are affectionate companions with huge personalities.

Akita

The Akita is a muscular, double-coated dog of ancient Japanese lineage famous for their dignity, courage, and loyalty.

The Basenji, Africa's 'Barkless Dog,' is a compact, sweet-faced hunter of intelligence and poise.

Tibetan Mastiff

Watchful, aloof, imposing, and intimidating: The ancient Tibetan Mastiff is the guardian dog supreme.
